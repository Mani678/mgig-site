Nigerian First

Proudly Nigerian. Powerfully Local.

Local content is not just a compliance requirement for MGIG — it is the foundation of who we are. We are committed to building Nigerian capacity, creating Nigerian jobs, and growing Nigerian enterprise.

Our Commitment

Local Content at the Heart of MGIG

MGIG was founded on the belief that Nigeria's oil and gas wealth should create lasting value for Nigerians. We are registered with the Nigerian Content Development and Monitoring Board (NCDMB) and committed to exceeding local content targets across every business segment.

Over 95% of our workforce are Nigerian nationals. We actively partner with Nigerian suppliers, manufacturers, and service providers — and we invest in building the skills and capacity of Nigerian engineers, technicians, and business professionals.

Programmes

How We Build Nigerian Capacity

🎓

Graduate Development Programme

Annual intake of Nigerian engineering and business graduates with structured 18-month rotational training across all divisions.

🔧

Technical Skills & Apprenticeship

Partnering with PTDF and technical institutes to train Nigerian technicians, welders, and field operators to international standards.

🏢

Nigerian Vendor Development

Preferential procurement from Nigerian-owned businesses, with dedicated vendor registration, prequalification support, and development programmes.

🤝

Community Enterprise Schemes

Supporting local entrepreneurs in host communities to access supply chain opportunities through structured mentorship and contract set-asides.

🏫

Education & Scholarship

Annual MGIG scholarships for students studying engineering, geosciences, and business in Nigerian universities.

📊

NCDMB Compliance & Reporting

Full compliance with Nigerian Oil and Gas Industry Content Development Act (NOGICD Act) with quarterly reporting to the NCDMB.

Community

Investing in Host Communities

MGIG operates a robust Community Development Programme (CDP) in all host communities across our areas of operation in the Niger Delta.

Education Support

School renovations, teacher training, and provision of learning materials across 12 communities.

Healthcare Access

Free medical outreach programmes and support for community health centres in the Niger Delta.

Clean Water

Borehole and water treatment projects providing clean water to over 8,000 community members.

Youth Employment

Priority employment for qualified youths from host communities in MGIG's operations.

Women Empowerment

Skills acquisition and micro-enterprise support for women in host communities.

Infrastructure

Road rehabilitation, electrification, and public facility upgrades in areas of operation.
