Who We Are

A Nigerian Energy Company Built for Africa

MGIG is a fully integrated oil and gas company headquartered in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. We operate across the entire energy value chain with deep local knowledge and international standards.

With over 1,200 professionals across Nigeria and West Africa, we combine expertise with commitment to deliver projects that matter to our clients, communities, and country.
