Who We Are

Built on Nigerian Soil. Built for Africa.

MGIG is a fully integrated oil and gas company rooted in Port Harcourt, driven by excellence, and committed to powering West Africa's energy future.

🎯

Our Mission

To deliver integrated, safe, and reliable oil and gas services across the value chain — creating value for our clients, communities, and country through technical excellence and local expertise.

🔭

Our Vision

To be Africa's most trusted and innovative indigenous energy company — setting the standard for local content excellence, sustainable operations, and pan-African energy leadership by 2030.

💎

Our Values

Integrity in every transaction. Safety above all else. Excellence in delivery. Respect for people and communities. Commitment to Nigerian development and sustainable growth.

Our Story

Two Decades of Energy Excellence

MGIG was founded in Port Harcourt with a clear mandate: to build a world-class indigenous oil and gas company that competes with the best while remaining deeply rooted in Nigerian values and local content principles.

Over two decades, we have grown from a focused oilfield services provider to a fully integrated energy group operating across exploration, production, engineering, trading, and supply chain — serving major IOCs, NOCs, and independent operators across West Africa.

Today, MGIG stands as a symbol of what Nigerian enterprise can achieve — delivering complex energy projects with the precision of global operators and the agility of a truly local partner.

2004
Company FoundedEstablished in Port Harcourt as an oilfield services provider serving the Niger Delta basin.
